Honoring Our
Heritage

The IBOM Festival is a one day cultural event that promotes unity among people of Ibom ancestry and other cultures in Nigeria through music, dance, art exhibitions, talk shows, and food exhibitions.

It gives people of Ibom ancestry in the southwest of Nigeria and neighboring countries near Lagos the chance to experience, appreciate, and participate in their cultural heritage.

This also coincides with AKWA IBOM state independence creation which usually falls on 23 September.

Vendor
Spotlight

At Ibom Festival Lagos, we're passionate about giving MSMEs the spotlight they deserve, from food to fashion, art to innovation. Our goal is to give MSMEs a high visibility platform to connect, grow, and sell. Over the years, we've seen cultural events open new doors for small businesses, and Ibom Fest Lagos is set to be one of the biggest yet.

What We Look for in Vendors:

  • Competitive Pricing

    Your products should offer attractive prices, discounts, or festival only deals that are better than open market rates.

  • Quality First

    We prioritize vendors who sell clean, original, and high quality products nothing worn, expired, or poorly made.

  • Unique Offerings

    The more distinctive your brand, the higher your chances. We love businesses that bring something fresh and culturally relevant.

  • Strong Online Presence

    Vendors are expected to promote their participation via social media for better brand reach and visibility.
